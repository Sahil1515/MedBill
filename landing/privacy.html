Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 11, 2026

MedBill ("we", "our", "us") is committed to protecting the privacy of our users. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use MedBill software and visit our website.

1. Information We Collect

Software (Desktop App): MedBill is an offline desktop application. All billing data, customer records, inventory, and reports are stored locally on your computer. We do not collect, transmit, or store any business data from the software.

Website: When you visit our website, we may collect standard server logs including IP address, browser type, and pages visited. We do not use tracking cookies or third-party analytics.

License Activation: When you activate a subscription, we collect your email address and a device identifier (hardware hash) solely to issue and validate your license key. This information is not sold or shared with third parties.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To send your subscription key and renewal reminders by email
  • To validate your license key during software activation
  • To respond to support requests
  • To send important product updates (you may opt out anytime)

3. Data Storage and Security

Your pharmacy's billing data is stored entirely on your local computer. We have no access to it. We recommend enabling the Auto-Backup feature to protect your data from hardware failure.

License and email data is stored in a secured database with industry-standard encryption. We retain this data only as long as your subscription is active plus a 90-day post-cancellation window.

4. Third-Party Services

We use Razorpay to process subscription payments. Razorpay's privacy policy applies to payment data. We do not store credit card or UPI details on our servers.

5. Your Rights

You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by emailing support@medbill.in. We will process the request within 7 business days.

6. Children's Privacy

MedBill is intended for use by pharmacy professionals. We do not knowingly collect data from individuals under 18.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the software after changes constitutes acceptance.

8. Contact

For privacy-related questions, contact us at support@medbill.in.
