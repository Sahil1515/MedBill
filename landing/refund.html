Refund Policy

Last updated: April 11, 2026

Short version: We offer a 7-day refund on any new subscription if you're not satisfied. Cancel anytime — no hassle.

1. Free Trial

MedBill offers a free 30-day trial with no credit card required. We encourage you to fully evaluate the software before subscribing.

2. Subscription Refunds

If you subscribe and are not satisfied, you may request a full refund within 7 days of your initial subscription payment. To request a refund:

  • Email support@medbill.in with your order ID
  • We will process the refund within 3–5 business days
  • The amount will be credited to your original payment method

3. Renewal Refunds

For monthly subscription renewals, you may request a refund within 48 hours of the renewal charge if you forgot to cancel. For annual renewals, a 7-day window applies.

4. Non-Refundable Cases

  • Refund requests made after the stated window has passed
  • Accounts found to be in violation of our Terms of Use
  • Partial-month refunds for mid-cycle cancellations (access continues until period end)

5. Cancellation

You can cancel your subscription at any time by emailing support. You will retain access until the end of your current paid period. We do not charge cancellation fees.

6. Contact

For refund requests or billing questions, email support@medbill.in or use our contact form. We aim to respond within one business day.
