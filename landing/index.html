FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Have more questions? Email us at support@medbill.in

Does it work without internet? ▼
Yes, 100% offline. All data is stored on your own computer. An internet connection is never required for billing — only to activate or renew your subscription key.
What happens after the 30-day trial? ▼
After 30 days the software will prompt you for a subscription key. Subscribe to a Monthly or Yearly plan, receive your key by email, and enter it in Settings → License to unlock.
Can I cancel my subscription anytime? ▼
Yes. Cancel anytime from your account or by emailing support. You retain access until the end of your current billing period. Your data stays on your computer — we don't delete it.
Is my billing data safe? ▼
Your data never leaves your computer — it's stored in a local database. The auto-backup feature copies it to a folder or USB drive of your choice every 24 hours.
Does it support barcode scanners? ▼
Yes. Any USB HID barcode scanner (the kind that shows up as a keyboard) works out of the box. No drivers needed.
Is GST billing supported? ▼
Yes. MedBill automatically applies CGST, SGST, and IGST based on the tax rate you set per medicine. GST-format invoices can be printed or shared via WhatsApp.
What payment methods do you accept? ▼
UPI, Net Banking, Debit/Credit Card via Razorpay. A GST invoice is emailed immediately after payment.
